**Tired of dark caves and hard-to-see areas in Minecraft?**  
Photon is a lightweight FullBright texture pack that makes caves, interiors, and dark areas fully visible while preserving vanilla colors.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** Photon acts as a FullBright texture pack. Some servers may consider this a cheat. Please consult server rules before use.

## Features
- FullBright lighting for caves, interiors, and underwater  
- Preserves vanilla colors and textures, no artificial glow  
- Client-side only; works in multiplayer if allowed by server rules  
- Fully compatible with vanilla-style gameplay  

---

## Requirements

| Branch       | Minecraft Version       | Forge Requirement            | Fabric Requirement           |
|-------------|------------------------|-----------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Legacy      | 1.6 → 1.12.2           | ⚠️ OptiFine required        | ❌ Not supported            |
| Legacy+     | 1.13 → 1.16.5          | ⚠️ OptiFine required        | ❌ Not supported   |
| Future      | 1.17 → 1.21.11         | Pack in development      | Pack in development      |


> 1.21+ versions are still being remade and are not yet available. The current 1.21+ version may not work with many releases.

---

## Why Photon?
Exploring dark areas shouldn’t be a struggle. Photon ensures you can see clearly everywhere while still feeling like vanilla Minecraft. No mods, no x-ray, just brighter, natural-looking visuals.

## Installation
1. Download the ZIP file.  
2. Place it in your Minecraft `resourcepacks` folder.  
3. Enable it from the Resource Pack menu.  

---

## Compatibility
- Works with all vanilla-based gameplay  
- Compatible with mods that don’t replace lighting or textures  
- Resource pack only, no mods or shaders required
